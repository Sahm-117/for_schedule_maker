Getting Started

🔐 Signing in

Open the app and enter your email address or Nigerian phone number (e.g. 08012345678) and password, then tap Sign in.

💡Support team members can self-register using the Create an account link. Admin and SOP Preparer accounts must be created by the programme admin.

📱 Creating a Support account

  1. Tap "Create an account"Found at the bottom of the login screen.
  2. Choose Email or Phone NumberPhone must be in local format: 08012345678 — not +234.
  3. Enter your full name and a passwordPassword must be at least 6 characters.
  4. Tap "Create Account"Your account is created as Support role. Ask your admin to assign your label group.

🔔 Enable notifications on first open

After logging in for the first time, you will see a prompt explaining what notifications you will receive. Tap Enable notifications then Allow in the browser dialog. You will miss schedule updates until this is done.

ℹ️The app tour will only start after you interact with the notification prompt — either enable or dismiss it. This ensures the tour doesn't compete with the prompt for your attention.

Roles & Access

Every user has one of three roles which determines what they can see and do.

👑

Admin

Programme Director
  • Create and manage weeks, days, activities
  • Approve or reject change requests
  • Manage all users and roles
  • Assign labels to users and activities
  • Post announcements to the team
  • Manage the Resource Hub
  • Export full programme schedule
  • Send push notifications to all users
  • Trigger Telegram daily digest
✏️

SOP Preparer

Programme Support Officer
  • View the full programme schedule
  • Submit change requests for admin approval
  • Track status of submitted changes
  • See rejection notes from admin
  • Receive push notifications
  • Export schedule to PDF
  • Export personal schedule (by label)
  • Access the Resource Hub
  • Install the app on their device
👁️

Support

Volunteer / Team Member
  • View schedule filtered to their label group
  • Export their personal schedule to PDF
  • Receive push notifications for updates
  • Access the Resource Hub
  • Install the app on their device
ℹ️Labels explained: Labels are group tags (e.g. "Tech Team", "Ushers", "Logistics"). Admin assigns labels to activities and to users. Support users only see activities matching their labels. Admin and SOP Preparer see everything — but can also have labels assigned for personal exports.

Viewing the Schedule

📅 Programme weeks

The left panel shows all programme weeks. Tap a week to load its schedule. Each week contains days, and each day contains timed activities with descriptions and team labels.

💡On mobile, the week selector is at the top and the schedule loads below. Scroll down to see activities for each day.

🏷️ Filtered view for Support users

If you are a Support member, the schedule automatically shows only activities assigned to your label group. You see only what is relevant to your team — no noise from other teams.

⚠️If your schedule is empty, your admin may not have assigned your label yet. Contact them to get set up.

🔴 Pending changes indicator

Admin SOP Preparer  A badge shows how many change requests are waiting. Admins see all pending changes; preparers see only their own.

Proposing & Tracking Changes

📝 How to submit a change request SOP Preparer

  1. Find the activity to editNavigate to the correct week and day.
  2. Tap the edit (pencil) iconOpens the change proposal form, pre-filled with current values.
  3. Make your updatesChange the title, time, description, or labels as needed.
  4. Submit for approvalYour change is saved as pending. The admin will review it.

✅ What happens next

  • 🟢 Approved — change applied to the live schedule immediately. You get a push notification.
  • 🔴 Rejected — you'll see a notification with the admin's reason in the Rejected Changes section.
  • ⏳ Pending — still awaiting admin review. No action needed from you.

Admin Controls

🗓️ Managing weeks, days & activities Admin only

  1. Create a weekTap + next to "Programme Weeks". Enter the week name and dates.
  2. Add daysOpen the week and tap "Add Day". Name it (e.g. Day 1 — Monday) and set the date.
  3. Add activitiesOpen a day and tap "Add Activity". Set title, time, description, and assign labels.

👥 User management Admin only

Via the Admin panel you can create users (any role), edit details, assign label groups, and deactivate accounts.

ℹ️Admins and SOP Preparers can also have labels assigned — useful so their personal export only shows their team's activities.

🏷️ Label management Admin only

Create labels like "Tech Team", "Ushers", "Security". Assign them to activities to indicate team involvement, and to users to filter what they see.

✅ Approving & rejecting changes Admin only

The Pending Changes panel shows all outstanding requests. Tap Approve to apply immediately, or Reject with an optional reason — the preparer is notified either way.

📢 Announcements Admin only

Send a message to the entire team. All users with push notifications enabled receive it on their device instantly.

📲 Telegram daily digest Admin only

Trigger a digest that sends tomorrow's activities to the team Telegram channel. Configure the channel in app settings.

🔑 Resetting a user's password Admin only

If a team member forgets their password, the admin can set a new one directly from User Management — no OTP or email flow needed.

  1. Open User ManagementTap the Admin button → User Management from the dashboard.
  2. Find the user and tap "Reset PW"An inline input appears in their row.
  3. Enter a new password (min 6 characters)You can share it with the user verbally or via WhatsApp.
  4. Tap "Save"The user can now log in with the new password immediately.
💡Ask the user to log in and mentally note their new password — there is no self-service password reset flow. Keep it simple.

📲 How to enable notifications

  1. Open the app after logging inA prompt appears on your first visit explaining what you will receive.
  2. Tap "Enable notifications"Your browser will ask you to confirm. Tap Allow.
  3. You're registeredNotifications arrive even when the app is closed, as long as the PWA is installed.
⚠️If you dismissed the prompt, go to your phone/browser Settings → Site Settings → Notifications and allow FOF IKD Ops manually.

🔕 Not receiving notifications?

  • Confirm you tapped Allow when the browser asked
  • Check that Do Not Disturb is not enabled
  • If you reinstalled the PWA, open the app once to refresh your subscription
  • Notifications go to your most recently registered device only

Resource Hub

📚 What is the Resource Hub?

A shared library of links and files the team needs — training guides, role documents, contact sheets, templates. All users can view; only admins can add or remove items.

📂 Opening the Hub

Find the Resource Hub card on the left sidebar. If new resources were added since your last visit, you will see an orange pulsing badge and a banner at the top of the dashboard. Tap either to open.

➕ Adding a resource Admin only

  1. Tap "Add" in the Resource HubButton appears in the top-right corner of the modal.
  2. Choose Link/URL or Upload FileFiles can be PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, images, or ZIP.
  3. Enter a title and optional description
  4. Toggle "Notify all users" if neededSends a push notification to everyone when you save.
  5. Tap "Add Resource"Appears immediately for all users.

Installing the App

📲 Why install?

FOF IKD Ops is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Installing it to your home screen means it opens full-screen like a native app, loads instantly, works offline, and delivers notifications reliably.

🍎 iPhone / iPad (Safari only)

  1. Open in SafariChrome on iOS does not support PWA install.
  2. Tap the Share buttonThe box-with-arrow icon at the bottom of Safari.
  3. Scroll to "Add to Home Screen" and tap it
  4. Tap "Add"The FOF IKD Ops icon appears on your home screen.

🤖 Android (Chrome)

  1. Open in ChromeA banner may appear automatically at the bottom.
  2. Tap "Install" or "Add to Home Screen"Or use the three-dot menu → Add to Home Screen.
  3. Confirm the install
💡Always open the app from the home screen icon — not the browser — for the best notification experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

I can't log in — what do I do?
Make sure you're using the correct email or phone number (format: 08012345678, not +234). If you forgot your password, contact your programme admin — they can reset it for you instantly from User Management. You'll be back in within a minute.
How does password reset work?
There is no self-service password reset. If you forget your password, message your programme admin. They open User Management, find your name, tap "Reset PW", set a new password, and share it with you. Log in with the new password — done.
My schedule is empty / I can't see any activities
Support users see only activities assigned to their label group. If nothing shows, your admin may not have assigned your label yet. Contact them. Admin and SOP Preparer users seeing an empty schedule should check their internet connection or refresh the page.
I submitted a change but it's not on the schedule yet
SOP Preparer changes go to the admin for approval first. The change only appears on the live schedule after the admin approves it. Check the Pending Changes section for status.
I'm not getting push notifications
Check that you tapped Allow when prompted. If you dismissed it, go to phone/browser Settings → Site Settings → Notifications and allow FOF IKD Ops. Also check Do Not Disturb is off. After reinstalling the PWA, open the app once to refresh your subscription.
The app shows old data
Pull down to refresh. If there's a "New version available" banner, tap Refresh to get the latest update. If data is still stale, check your internet connection.
Can I use the app on multiple devices?
Yes. Push notifications will go to your most recently registered device. If you switch phones, open the app on the new device and enable notifications to move the subscription over.
How do I see the full schedule, not just my team's?
Support users see a filtered view based on their assigned labels. To see the full schedule, your admin would need to upgrade your role to SOP Preparer or Admin. Speak to your programme admin.
"Export My Schedule" — who is this for?
All roles can use this. It exports only the activities assigned to your label group, making it perfect for printing your personal programme sheet. If you have no labels assigned, it exports the full schedule.
