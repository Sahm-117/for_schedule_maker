Getting Started

🔐 Signing in

Open the app and enter your email address or Nigerian phone number (e.g. 08012345678) and password, then tap Sign in. After signing in you land on your own home page — admins see the operations dashboard, support members see their personal home.

💡Support team members can self-register using the Create an account link. Admin accounts are created by the programme admin.

📱 Creating a Support account

  1. Tap "Create an account"Found at the bottom of the login screen.
  2. Choose Email or Phone NumberPhone must be in local format: 08012345678 — not +234.
  3. Enter your full name and a passwordPassword must be at least 6 characters.
  4. Tap "Create Account"You're created as a Support member. Your admin assigns you to a group and activity tags.

🧭 The guided tour & notifications

On your first visit you'll be asked to enable notifications, then a short in-app walkthrough highlights the key buttons on each page. You can skip the tour at any time and re-trigger it later from the ? help button on most pages.

How It's Organised

The whole app is built around one simple structure. Understanding it makes everything else click into place.

🏗️ Cohort → Group → Participant

Cohort→ Groups→ Participants
  • Cohort — one run of the programme (e.g. "Cohort 9"), made up of numbered weeks. One cohort is "active" at a time.
  • Group — a small group inside a cohort (e.g. "Group 16"). Each group is led by exactly one Support member.
  • Participant — a person in a small group. Each participant belongs to one group.
ℹ️Follow-up contacts are different. Contacts are prospective people being invited/registered. They are tracked separately from participants and can be assigned to a Support member to call and follow up.

🏷️ Activity tags

Activities on the schedule carry tags (e.g. "Prayer", "Class", "WhatsApp post"). Support members are assigned tags, and their schedule shows only the activities matching their tags — so everyone sees just what's relevant to them.

Roles & Access

There are two main sides to the app. Your role decides which one you see when you log in.

👑

Admin

Programme Coordinator
  • Set up cohorts, weeks & the full schedule
  • Add participants & build groups
  • Assign Support leads & activity tags
  • Publish the schedule to Support
  • Oversee attendance, prayers & faith projects
  • Manage follow-ups & onboarding
  • Review change approvals, post announcements
  • Manage users, resources & settings
🤝

Support

Small-Group Leader
  • See their week-at-a-glance home page
  • View their schedule (once published)
  • Mark their own activities done
  • Run their small group: faith projects & prayers
  • Take attendance for their group
  • Onboard their participants step-by-step
  • Work their assigned follow-up contacts
  • Manage their profile & reminders
ℹ️An SOP Preparer role also exists for staff who help build the schedule and submit change requests for admin approval. It sits on the Admin side of the app with a narrower set of permissions.
For Coordinators

👑 Admin Side

Everything below is for admins. This is where the programme is set up and run: cohorts, the schedule, the people, and all the oversight tools. Support members do not see these screens.

A

Admin Dashboard & Navigation

Your landing page. The operations snapshot shows pending approvals, resource count, number of Support members and announcements at a glance. Below it: today's activity snapshot with completion status, the approval queue, and recent announcements.

🗂️ The grouped sidebar Admin

Admin navigation is grouped into collapsible sections so it stays tidy:

  • Engagement — Follow-ups, Onboarding

A1

Cohorts & Weeks

🎓 Managing cohorts Admin

On the Cohorts page you create and manage each run of the programme. One cohort is active at a time; everyone works against the active cohort. You can duplicate a cohort to reuse its structure, archive old ones, and permanently delete a cohort you no longer need.

📅 Weeks & week titles Admin

A cohort is made up of numbered weeks. You can add a week, give each week a descriptive title, duplicate a week's activities into a new week, and delete a specific week. Weeks always stay in numeric order.

💡Duplicating a week copies its activities — a fast way to set up a new week that's similar to a previous one, then tweak the differences.
A2

Participants & Groups

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Participants Admin

Add participants individually or import them into the active cohort. Search and filter by name, phone, or group, assign each person to a group, and manage their status (active / archived).

👥 Groups Admin

Create the small groups inside a cohort and assign one Support member to lead each group. Manage who's in each group, and filter to spot groups that don't yet have a Support lead assigned.

🔀 Allocation Admin

The Allocation screen helps you place participants who aren't in a group yet. Drag them into groups manually, or use auto-distribute to spread unallocated participants evenly across groups in one click.

A3

Schedule & Publishing

🗓️ Building the schedule Admin

Add and edit activities for each day of each week. Every activity has a time, a description (with rich text — bold, italic, bullet and numbered lists), and one or more activity tags. You can filter the schedule by support tag or by a specific Support user to preview exactly what they'll see.

💡WhatsApp and Telegram are shown with their icons (and labels) right inside the activity text, so post instructions read clearly.

🚦 Publishing — the visibility gate Admin

The schedule starts in Draft. Support members cannot see any activities until you publish the schedule for the active cohort. Use the publish toggle at the top of the Schedule page — you'll be asked to confirm. You can unpublish at any time to take it back into draft.

⚠️If Support members report a blank "Schedule not published yet" message, that's expected until you publish. Publish when the week's plan is final.

📝 Approvals Admin

When an SOP Preparer proposes a change (add, edit, or delete an activity), it lands in the Approvals queue. Review each request and Approve to apply it to the live schedule, or Reject with an optional reason — the preparer is notified either way.

📈 Activity overview Admin · SOP Preparer

A completion breakdown per activity, per day — see at a glance who has marked an activity done and who hasn't, with filters for day, support tag, and individual Support user.

A4

Attendance, Prayers & Faith Projects

✅ Attendance Admin

Mark each participant Present, Late, or Absent for any week. Filter by group to review summaries and per-person status across the cohort.

🙏 Group prayers Admin

A matrix of groups by week showing which group completed their prayer and who the prayer focus was, with a trend strip summarising weekly completion across all groups.

🌱 Faith projects Admin

Each participant has a faith project (a personal goal). View and edit any participant's project and track its status through the stages: Not drafted → Under refinement → Approved.

A5

Follow-ups & Onboarding

📞 Follow-ups Admin

Manage follow-up contacts (prospective participants). Track reply, call, and registration statuses; assign contacts to an owner to work; send templated messages; multi-select contacts; and raise/resolve issues. A breakdown dashboard summarises progress and what's still open.

🚀 Onboarding Admin

Track how far each group is through onboarding (percentage of participants fully onboarded), manage the message templates Support members use, set up onboarding coordinators, and watch the live activity feed of onboarding events.

A6

Users, Announcements, Resources & Settings

👤 Users Admin

Add users, assign roles (Admin, SOP Preparer, Support), and manage each Support member's activity tags. You can also reset a user's password directly from here — no email or OTP flow.

  1. Open UsersFrom the Administration section of the sidebar.
  2. Find the user and choose Reset passwordAn inline field appears in their row.
  3. Enter a new password (min 6 chars) and saveShare it with the user; they can log in immediately.

📢 Announcements Admin

Send a message to the team. Everyone with notifications enabled receives it on their device, and it appears in their in-app announcements feed.

📚 Resources Admin

Manage the shared Resource Hub — add links or upload files (PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, images, ZIP), with an option to notify everyone when you add something. All users can view; only admins add or remove.

⚙️ Settings Admin

Manage workspace settings, including the daily Telegram digest — toggle it on and send tomorrow's activities to the team Telegram channel manually when needed.

For Small-Group Leaders

🤝 Support Side

Everything below is for Support members. Your screens are focused on your group and your own activities — clean and uncluttered. You won't see any of the admin setup tools.

S1

Home & My Schedule

🏠 Your Home page Support

A week-at-a-glance: where you are in the cohort (e.g. "Week 1 of 10"), how many activities you have today, your next group prayer, faith-project progress for your group, and the latest announcements. Quick links jump you straight to your group, resources, follow-ups and profile.

📅 My Schedule Support

Your activities for the week, filtered to your activity tags so you only see what's yours. Switch between Today, Next Days, and Full Week.

⚠️If you see "Schedule not published yet", your coordinator is still finalising the plan. It will appear as soon as they publish it.

☑️ Mark activities done Support

Tap Mark done when you finish an activity — it saves instantly. Tap again to undo. Your admin sees this completion roll up in the Activity overview.

S2

My Group

🌱 Faith projects Support

For each participant in your group you can view and update their faith project status (Not drafted → Under refinement → Approved) and keep their goal moving forward.

🙏 Weekly group prayer Support

Pick a prayer focus for the week from your participants and mark the group prayer as done. This feeds the admin's group prayers matrix.

📋 Quick actions Support

Copy a participant's phone number in one tap to message or call them quickly.

S3

Attendance & Onboarding

✅ Take attendance Support

Choose a week and mark each of your group members Present, Late, or Absent. You can add a private note on any participant — anything worth remembering.

🚀 Onboard your participants Support

Work through each participant's onboarding checklist: Contacted → Added to group → Introduction done → Venue acknowledged. Send a ready-made welcome message and copy their number right from the screen. Each saved step shows a spinner only on the box you tapped, so you can update several quickly.

ℹ️If you're an onboarding coordinator, you'll also see a section for sending coordinator-to-support onboarding prompts.
S4

My Follow-ups & Profile

📞 My Follow-ups Support

The contacts assigned to you. Right after each message or call, update their reply, call, and registration status — the app applies sensible cascades automatically (e.g. marking someone Registered also marks them Replied). Send templated WhatsApp messages and flag issues when a number is wrong or a contact needs attention.

👤 Profile & reminders Support

View your details and activity tags, and set how you want to receive reminders and notifications.

Installing the App

📲 Why install?

FOF IKD Ops is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Installing it to your home screen means it opens full-screen like a native app, loads instantly, and delivers notifications reliably. An Install banner appears in-app while you're using it in a browser — and disappears once installed.

🤖 Android (Chrome)

  1. Open in Chrome and log inYou'll see the in-app Install banner. Chrome may also show its own install card at the top.
  2. Tap "Install"Or use Chrome's three-dot menu → Install app / Add to Home screen.
  3. ConfirmThe FOF IKD Ops icon appears on your home screen.

🍎 iPhone / iPad (Safari only)

  1. Open in SafariChrome on iOS cannot install PWAs.
  2. Tap the Share buttonThe box-with-arrow icon.
  3. Tap "Add to Home Screen" → Add
💡Always open the app from the home-screen icon — not the browser — for the most reliable notifications.

Frequently Asked Questions

I can't log in — what do I do?
Use the correct email or phone number (format: 08012345678, not +234). Forgot your password? Contact your programme admin — they reset it instantly from Users. You'll be back in within a minute.
My schedule is empty / says "not published yet"
Support members only see activities after the admin publishes the schedule for the active cohort. Until then you'll see the "not published yet" message — that's normal. If it stays empty after publishing, your activity tags or group may not be set; contact your admin.
How does the Cohort → Group → Participant structure work?
A Cohort is one run of the programme made of numbered weeks. Inside a cohort are Groups; each group is led by one Support member and contains Participants. Follow-up contacts are separate — they're prospective people being invited, not participants.
What's the difference between a Participant and a Follow-up contact?
A Participant is someone already in a small group, taking part in the programme. A Follow-up contact is a prospective person being called/registered. Contacts can be assigned to a Support member to work; once registered, they may become participants.
I marked an activity done by mistake
Tap "Mark done" again to undo it. It saves instantly each way.
How do I publish or unpublish the schedule? Admin
On the Schedule page, use the publish toggle at the top and confirm. Publishing makes the schedule visible to Support; unpublishing returns it to draft and hides it again.
I'm not getting push notifications
Check you tapped Allow when prompted. If you dismissed it, go to phone/browser Settings → Site Settings → Notifications and allow FOF IKD Ops. Make sure Do Not Disturb is off. After reinstalling the app, open it once to refresh your subscription. Notifications go to your most recently registered device only.
The Install banner keeps showing
That's by design — in a browser the banner reminds you on each visit. It disappears for good once you install the app to your home screen and open it from there.
The app shows old data
Pull down to refresh. If a "New version available" banner appears, tap Refresh to update. If data is still stale, check your internet connection.
